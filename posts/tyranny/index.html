Tyranny of Language

Salma Tawfik

2024/03/16

In the lexicon of life, a grim tableau,
Where words can wound, and spirits low.

In every phrase, a dagger’s thrust,
Power’s hand, in language’s rust.

In boardroom meetings, where suits convene,
Language dictates who reigns supreme.

The one who speaks with confidence, bold,
Controls the narrative, as stories unfold.

On streets where whispers fade to naught,
The silenced masses, battles fought.

Their voices stifled, dreams suppressed,
By language’s tyranny, they’re oppressed..

In whispered conversations, behind closed doors,
Language reveals secrets, it keeps scores.

Each word a weapon, each silence a shield,
In the battle for power, truths revealed.

Char O’Brien // Salma Tawfik // Lucas Mei
